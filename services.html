Services

What I take on, and how it runs.

I work with a small number of clients at a time, usually end-to-end from a rough idea through to a shipped, maintained interface.

Website development

Full builds from a design or a rough brief — semantic HTML, handcrafted CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, no framework overhead unless the project genuinely needs one.

01

Responsive design

Built for the smallest screen first, not shrunk down from desktop afterward — separate interaction models for touch and pointer, not just breakpoints.

Landing pages

Focused, single-purpose pages for a launch, a campaign, or a waitlist — built fast, measured honestly, and easy to hand off to whoever runs the campaign after me.

UI / UX design

Interface design that accounts for real states — empty, loading, error — not just the happy path shown in a polished mockup.

Performance optimization

Auditing and fixing what's actually slow — render-blocking assets, layout shift, unnecessary JavaScript — with numbers measured before and after, not assumed.

Full-stack services — coming as I take them on

I'm extending into lightweight backend work (APIs, simple data layers) so I can offer full-stack builds for smaller products. Not offered as a formal service yet — ask if your project needs it and I'll tell you honestly whether I'm ready.
